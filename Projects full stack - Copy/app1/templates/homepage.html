About

  • This website has been developed with the intentiion to procure and distribute fresh products.
  • The website mainly focuses on the buying and selling of homegrown agricultural products.
  • As this is more of a direct approach to sell and buy products,this method eliminates the additional cost incured due to transportation and middlemen which brings in more profit for manufacturers and saves the extra charges for consumers.
  • This could turnout huge relief for farmers who face poverty due to the low selling price assigned to their products as part of the normal selling approach.
The BUY option has been provided so that consumers can obtain homegrown agricultural products ranging from large scale to small scale at reduced price.
The SELL option provides the farmers and manufacturers with customer options,a direct approach which enable them to obtain greater profits.
